# Titanium battcar bolts

Original Harken battcar bolts are 1/4"-20, 2" long, 1" smooth, 1" thread, plus locking nuts.
I have six of them.

Upgrading to Grade 5 Ti bolts.
Reasons: original ones are old and corroded, plus they’re much longer than need to be.
Another reason: the smooth surface of these bolts isn’t long enough, some of the thread is exposed and may chafe the mainsail.

Gonna get 6 × [1/4"-20 × 1-3/4" hex head bolt from Ti64.com](https://www.ti64.com/product-p/4684.htm).
Additionally, 6 × [1/4"-20 hex nylon insert lock nut from Ti64.com](https://www.ti64.com/product-p/1048.htm).

All in all, for less than $100 I’m going to have my battcar’s bolts taken care of for the rest of my (or boat’s) life.
Unless I lose the mast, that is.
