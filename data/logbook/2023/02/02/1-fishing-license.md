# Florida fishing license

I think it's beyond stupid that people need to pay $54 every year to be able to fish in Florida, but here we are.

How to get it:

1. Go to [license.gooutdoorsflorida.com](https://license.gooutdoorsflorida.com)
1. Click on a barely visible button that says "Create Account"
1. Fill out the sign up form that looks exactly like the login form
1. Click on "Yes, create account" button
1. Fill out the full sign up form, that looks like a real sign up form (*)
1. Click on "Create Customer"
1. Purchase saltwater fishing license for 1 year ($47 + tax at the time of writing)

\* - The SSN field was a total disaster.
Tried to register as an intl customer, but the problem turned out to be in the field getting masked.
Use "Inspect HTML" in your browser, and change the "value" attribute of that input to your SSN
before submitting the form.
